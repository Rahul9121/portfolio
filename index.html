Research Paper

AI-Enhanced Biodiversity Risk Assessment for Hospitality Sector Compliance in New Jersey

Rahul Ega.

BiodivProScope is a location-based risk assessment tool designed to help hotels in New Jersey comply with biodiversity regulations. It analyzes ecological threats—like endangered species, invasive organisms, and pollution—within a 5-mile radius using geospatial data and AI-enhanced logic. The platform offers interactive maps, tailored mitigation strategies, and downloadable reports, making environmental compliance both accessible and actionable for hotel managers.
